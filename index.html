Business Case Submission #1

Evaluation of Initial Research

Thunderbolts Consulting · June 12 2025

Purpose & Objectives

  • Clarify Gen Z employment decision factors
  • Assess Canadian Tire’s employer brand & perception gaps
  • Benchmark competitor recruitment tactics
  • Recommend cost-effective, scalable HR strategies

Audience Snapshot

  • Primary: Bob Hakeem, CHRO – decision-maker
  • Secondary: Store & regional HR teams – implementers
  • Gatekeepers / Observers: Executive assistant, media, current staff
  • Assumed stance: Neutral–friendly, time-pressured

Environmental Factors

  • Retail talent shortages & high turnover
  • Gen Z values: flexibility, purpose, inclusivity
  • Competitors investing in modern employer branding
  • Digital-first candidate journeys (TikTok, LinkedIn, Indeed)
  • Cost constraints & internal change readiness

Key Questions Guiding Research

Who?  What?  When?  Where?  Why?  How?
  • Where do Gen Z candidates search (#JobTok, quick-apply)?
  • Why do they leave within a year?
  • How can we streamline the funnel (< 60-second application)?

    Ganguli et al., 2022
