wow number

  • $10,000 – average cost to replace one frontline retail employee, so every Gen Z hire you keep is a five‑figure saving (McKinsey, 2024)
  • 27 % – share of the OECD workforce that will be Gen Z by 2025, turning today’s “next‑gen” into tomorrow’s core staff (World Economic Forum, 2022)
  • 70 % of Gen Zers upskill every week (and nearly two‑thirds do it on their own time) – a workforce hungry for growth (Deloitte, 2025)
  • 52 % of Gen Z live paycheque‑to‑paycheque, yet 89 % say a sense of purpose is essential to job satisfaction – meaning you must offer both security and meaning to win their loyalty (Deloitte, 2025)
  • 31 % of Gen Z workers say they’ll switch employers within the next two years —an attrition clock already ticking (Deloitte, 2025)
  • 72 % of Gen Z employees have thought about quitting jobs that lack flexible‑work options —rigid schedules are a deal‑breaker (LinkedIn, 2025)
